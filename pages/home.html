Welcome to Flight Club!

Flight Club is an orbital launch simulator and data visualiser.

Create any orbital mission conceivable by choosing a launch site, building a launch vehicle, and creating a flight profile from events including Engine Ignitions and Cutoffs, Stage Separations, Fairing Deployments, even set when to begin calculating Landing Burns for the most efficient landing (but don't worry, Flight Club will do the calculations!).

For a quick tutorial on how to build launch vehicles and how to create successful flight profiles, click "Run Tutorial" below. Otherwise, get building!
